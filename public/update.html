Week 1: Languages of Code

Last week was a great start to the summer. Campers showed a great deal of interest as we began learning primarily HTML, CSS and a little bit of JavaScript. It was really exciting to see the pages each camper came up with using CodePen (a coding playground).
I encourage you to ask your camper to log into their personal accounts at home to share their master pieces!!
